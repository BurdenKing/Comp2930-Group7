Services

ParkAway is a CrowdSourcing solution to the issue of parking. ParkAway revolves around taking user input from the community to show available parking lots and comments about their status..

The main features include being able to see the state of parking lots and the comments about them. Users can also earn points from being active in the community. These points can be redeemed for weekly prizes and gifts. Users will be able to sign up for ParkAway. This allows other users to view their profile, and give them the ability to leave updates and comments.

Community input is the key to making ParkAway effective. It shows users the states of parking lots and it also results in ParkAway having an effect on the community. People can get involved and show their commitment in the community.
